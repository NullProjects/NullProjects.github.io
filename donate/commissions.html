Donations

Donations will be geared towards helping with funding on asset production and fees we have to pay to make things happen whether it is for advertisement, paying employee's, paying for services to expand storage and distribution and even server fees. The donations vary, but will help for each the main area we will be focusing on is the crowdfunding section with patreon and that will help commit more money to keep things active and going progressing at a steady pace regardless how many people are working on the project at the given time. If you would like to donate you can either click here or click the donate with paypal button at the top of the page or below. We will be working on our current images in the near future to make a more customized look and will be adding in comission based projects within the application form we came up with.

I will be accepting commissions and requests, however to deviate from the current project I am working on I will do so depending on the type of project you are wanting.

Donations
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Hey everyone, there are multiple prize incentives out there for donating some incentives get you more bang for your buck... the incentives are listed below as well as the milestones. Keep in mind though, not everything is going to charity, some of it will be towards the prize giveaways as well as well as helping the streamers out to keep up to date hardware and video game content throughout the entire donation run. The $10,000 goal will be an adition and total from patreon and actual donations and we will be updating the tracker as to what was recieved thus far between all both of the locations. It might look intimidating but it isn't really, we are going for a large enough donation run to keep the donations going and event going as long as we can so people have a chance to contribute to help out the organizations we are donating too.

So a little information about this donation run, as you may look down below you might say "hey this math may seem a little off for a fundraiser event". Well the explanation for that is simple, what we are trying for is donation runs split into two parts. One part of it will be split between 2-3 fundraiser organizations. The fundraiser events will go to a foundation for Cancer (St Judes), MS (National Multiple Sclerosis Society), and Spinal Stenosis (World Spine Care). The second part of it will go to a 25%-30% (not set in stone) pot geared for donation prize givaways so really the funding the prizes will come from the fundraiser event so you win 2 different ways. Whatever is left of the 25%-30% split off will go to the streamers as a thank you for participating in the fundraiser movement for these three foundations. Streamers will be streaming to their own channels though even they are participating in the event. There will be a section added onto the website containing information and actual streams embeded within the website so people can watch and talk to the streamers within their own channel.

This way streamers can stream to their own channels if they choose to do so so they can maintain their own follower base. All we ask is they refer to the stream and area or information as to let people know the event is indeed an ongoing event. The reason for giving a portion to the streamers is content, or power. As we all know, getting new content to stream as streamers cost's money... sometimes its money that we do not have at current to get the latest video games or hardware to play the latest games the portion to the streamers is meant to help out with this particular issue so we can provide enjoyment for however long this fundraiser event lasts. Our goal, keep it going for at least a year... but possibly LONGER than that that way we can raises as much money as we possibly can as streamers to these organizations that are dedicated to helping people, in short... we will try to make it our job to help these organizations succede.

Crowdfunding

As we all know crowdfundiung makes it possible for indie developers to strive and be able to pay employees or find a way to produce the work they are working on. This particular crowdfunding campaign will focus on both of those aspects and at current we are looking for people and looking for ways for people to join uis in our venture to a video game development studio. The crowdfunding inititive that we are going for will get you a chance at sneak peek features of products that will be listed within the Null Projects website as well as the NGNTV and SZE websites as well when they become live and fully functional. However the crowdfunding campaign is indeed currently active at https://www.patreon.com/nullprojects or click the patreon link button above or below to go to the actual patreon page. Patreon is our choice to use for this crowdfunding venture. For a while we were exploring other alternatives to go with and comparing different areas we can set up on such as kickstarter, gofundme, indiegogo and other various platforms but patreon apealed to us most because of the freedom and flexability on timeline based events.

The reason this was a main concern for us the timeline based events is because we are pretty much babies to the industry entering a world and getting a secure foothold out the door and slowly becoming known. A way we will achieve doing this through advertisement of our own is setting up connection to the patreon place while we do commission based work. Thats right, you heard it comission based work.. its a way where we can advertise ourselves and make ourselves known and heard through the coming months & even years.
