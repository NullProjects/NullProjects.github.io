Donations

Donations will be geared towards helping with funding on asset production and fees we have to pay to make things happen whether it is for advertisement, paying employee's, paying for services to expand storage and distribution and even server fees. The donations vary, but will help for each the main area we will be focusing on is the crowdfunding section with patreon and that will help commit more money to keep things active and going progressing at a steady pace regardless how many people are working on the project at the given time. If you would like to donate you can either click here or click the donate with paypal button at the top of the page or below. Commissions

I will be accepting commissions and requests, however to deviate from the current project I am working on I will do so depending on the type of project you are wanting.

3D Artwork


- 3D Artwork: $25 1x Object Size Restriction is furniture size | No UV Mapping
- 3D Artwork: $50 1x Building | No UV Mapping
- 3D Artwork: $100 1x Scene or consisting 4 objects + $10 per aditional object after 4 objects are made. No UV Mapping |

So hypathetically if you wanted to do 3 objects and a building without UV Mapping, that would be a total of $125, With UV Mapping that would be $185 House being included as an object.
