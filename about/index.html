Team Members TOS

This website is under construction, please check back later to see if & when the website is ready :)

We are a software development team as well as a video game development team. While there may be a shop there will be an open source side to all of this. The projects vary from back end of the video game functionalty, server packages and even miscalnious projects. While this website is on a github hosted area it will be a listing of on-going projects open source and none open source. The non open sourced projects will be listed under the shop feature, the open sourced ones... well that will be listed under the development feature.

There are other aspects to this website as well, you can find more about the official team members in charge of this website, their experience and method of recruitment for other projects as well if applicable. That information can be found here. Null Projects in general however is the back end team behind a company which will remain undisclosed for now. This may be the case at the mean time being undisclosed and all hush hush. However the only thing that is ready or will be ready in the mean time for announcement is the Null Projects back end of things, or rather the open source development and progress made with everything.

If you wish to join us in the development of the company or wish to join in on the team please contact us and we will let you know when we are ready to accept applications. However please keep in mind while there is a career option to apply for it is far from ready to be apply for that. Open source development will strive on from donation aspects only and each individual team member can carry their own paypal donation method way and their own personalized website. However the projects that are non open sourced and off located onto another website and set of shops that are indeed listed on this website for reference only are of propiatary nature. This means the team member that is involved in one of those projects will recieve payment in agreement too from the ammount of sales accumulated during that month or a period time frame set. If there is $0 accumulation of sales of those propriatary projects then both Null Projects and Individualized team members will get $0 in return. However if it were $1, Null Projects would get 50% of the anual income which would be $0.50 and each team member will be depenedent on how many team members are actually involved and split evenly between the remaining $0.50.

The 50% going to Null Projects will help pay for numerous of things. Licenses for products used, royalties due, website fees, and hosting fees of numerous nature. There will be more details on what Null Projects is and what we are about at a later time but this will hopefully let you in on what some of our goals are. If you would like to help fund this ongoing project and help build us up then we invite you to do so via our donation page which will entail methods of donating to us, through our partner shop, paypal or even gofundme. There is also another method of donation as well, we will host broadcasts every now and then and you can donate via that method as way through twitch or picarto.
